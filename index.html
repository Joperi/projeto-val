Jett

Han Sunwoo (Korean: 한선우) is a radiant from Seoul, South Korea, "Jett" with her ability with the wind and her knifes, was the 10th Agent to join the VALORANT PROTOCOL, and is among the youngest of its members.

Sova

Sasha Novikov (Russian: Саша Новиков) is an archer from the town of Severomorsk in Murmansk Oblast, Russia, "Sova" with his bow, special arrows and a extremely accurate shots, he became an agent for the VALORANT PROTOCOL as its 6th recruit.

Sage

Wei Ling Ying (Chinese: 魏玲瑩) is a radiant monk from Shaanxi province in China, "Sage" is a healer whose capacity to turn the tide of battle is immeasurable. She is the 7th Agent to join the VALORANT PROTOCOL.

Killjoy

Klara Böhringer is an inventor from Germany. "Killjoy" with her technological equipments such as turrets, bombs and robots, she was the 4th agent to join the VALORANT PROTOCOL.

Gekko

Mateo Armendáriz De la Fuente is a radiant from Los Angeles, California, USA, "Gekko" is the owner of Dizzy, Mosh, Thrash and Wingman, a crew of creatures known as radivores with the ability to transform from their primary globule state into mimicked animalistic forms, he is the 22nd agent to join the VALORANT PROTOCOL.

Clove

Clove is an agent from Edinburgh, Scotland, United Kingdom, "Clove" is a troublemaker that makes mischief for enemies in both the heat of combat and the cold of death. The young immortal keeps foes guessing, even from beyond the grave, their return to the living only ever a moment away, being the 25th agent to join the VALORANT PROTOCOL.

Iso

Li Zhao Yu (Chinese: 李兆宇) is a radiant from Chongqing, China, "Iso" is a hitman with the ability to transmute ambient, intangible radianite energy into solid, durable constructs, he is 24th agent to join the VALORANT PROTOCOL.
